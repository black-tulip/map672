UAE Triangle

Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Al Ain

Map authored by Nils Lewis

This map shows three of the main cities in the UAE with population data attached.

Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and Al Ain form a natural triangle bracketing much of the population of the the UAE. The roads between them were some of the first paved roads in the UAE built in the 1970s, and the older generation today still remembers going between them by camel or landrover over a sea of dunes, or between Abu Dhabi and Dubai by wooden dhow.
